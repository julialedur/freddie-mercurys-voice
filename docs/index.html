FREDDIE
MERCURY'S

VOICE VISUALIZED



Freddie Mercury, British singer-songwriter and lead vocalist of rock band Queen, defied stereotypes and shattered convention to become one of the most beloved entertainers on the planet. With timeless hits like Bohemian Rhapsody and Don't Stop Me Now, his unique stage presence and four-octave vocal range has left a resounding legacy that provokes and inspires listeners and musicians alike.

We will explore various dimensions of Freddie Mercury's songs using audio analysis. We hypothesize that there exists a correlation between features of his songs and song popularity. We also investigate how much more powerful his voice is compared to other great vocalists.
